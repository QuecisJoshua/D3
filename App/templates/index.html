Correlations Discovered Between Poverty & Lack of Healthcare


Analysis: There is a correlation between % Poverty and Lack of healthcare. Two-thirds of the states in United States show that people who are in poverty also lack proper means to care for their health. Some of the States like MA and TX are outliers. MA shows higher coverage of healthcare even though there is high level of poverty in their state. TX, on the other hand, has higher amount of people without healthcare coverage even though the poverty level is low. As expected, most of the data does show the correlation.
